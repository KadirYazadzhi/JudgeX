Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our platform. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to us.

What programming languages are supported on your platform?

+

Our platform supports a wide range of programming languages, including Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, TypeScript, and many more. You can choose your preferred language to solve coding challenges.

How does the real-time feedback system work?

+

When you submit your code, our real-time feedback system immediately evaluates it against a set of test cases. You'll receive instant feedback on any errors or issues, along with suggestions for improvement.

Can I track my progress over time?

+

Yes, our platform includes detailed progress tracking. You can view your performance history, see which challenges you've completed, and track your improvement over time.

Is there a community or forum where I can ask for help?

+

Absolutely! We have a vibrant community forum where you can ask questions, share knowledge, and collaborate with other learners and experienced programmers.

Are there different difficulty levels for the coding challenges?

+

Yes, we offer challenges at various difficulty levels, from Basic to Insane. You can select the level that matches your experience and gradually tackle more challenging problems as you improve.
